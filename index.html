Our Team
Niranjan Orkat
A Generalist Engineer with 5 years of industry experience spanning Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Architecture, and Computational Art. He writes academic philosophy, focusing on Philosophy of Language, Psychoanalysis, and Aesthetics. Niranjan is interested in doing work—capital N New—work that resists assimilation into its field, invariant of the domain.
Harleen Dhunda
A graduate in Psychology & English Literature from Kristu Jayanti College, Harleen is a UX designer and researcher who crafts digital experiences. She has worked across Ed-tech, health, and game design. Drawn to where art meets social impact, Harleen weaves research and emotion into her work. Also a musician, writer, and speaker, she believes in moving fluidly between ideas and expression.
George Panicker
A Bangalore-based practitioner working across new media art, creative tech, computational design, and Earth system science. Engaging with immersive, synthetic, computational, and moist media, George’s practice seeks to be a virtual grounding for futures that include both human and nonhuman life. Currently an artist/researcher in residence at the Hertz Lab, ZKM Karlsruhe.
